# LiftBook

**Your ultimate companion for personalized workouts and precise performance tracking.**

## Key Features

### Complete Exercise Database
- **873 exercises** pre-loaded with detailed instructions
- Advanced search and filters by muscles, equipment, and categories
- Custom exercises to adapt your routine

### Workout Sessions
- Create custom workouts or use templates
- Built-in rest timer with notifications
- Real-time tracking of sets, weight, and repetitions
- Intuitive interface for quick logging

### Progress Tracking
- Complete history of all your workouts
- Progress charts and personal records
- Total volume and detailed statistics
- Analysis of your strengths and areas for improvement

### Advanced Personalization
- Reusable workout templates
- Customizable settings (units, timers, haptic feedback)
- Built-in multilingual support
- Interface adapted to your preferences

Transform your fitness routine with professional tracking that adapts to your level and goals!
