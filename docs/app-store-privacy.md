# App Store Privacy Answers

Use these answers for the LiftBook v1 App Store Connect privacy section.

## Data Not Linked to the User

- Device ID
  - Purpose: Analytics
  - Tracking: No
  - Source: TelemetryDeck analytics

- Product Interaction
  - Purpose: Analytics
  - Tracking: No
  - Source: TelemetryDeck analytics events

- Crash Data
  - Purpose: App Functionality
  - Tracking: No
  - Source: Sentry crash reporting

- Performance Data
  - Purpose: App Functionality
  - Tracking: No
  - Source: Sentry performance tracing at low sample rate

## Not Collected by LiftBook v1

- Health and Fitness data entered in the app is stored locally and is not intentionally transmitted.
- Contact information, location, contacts, photos, audio, purchases, advertising data, and sensitive information are not collected by LiftBook v1.
- Data is not used for third-party advertising, developer advertising or marketing, or tracking.

## Privacy Links

- Privacy Policy: `https://dinoboros.github.io/LiftBook/privacy/`
- Privacy Choices: not required for v1.
